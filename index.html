HOME!

Hello, my name is Evan and welcome to my website! This is part of a project for my CIS 260 course at Wilbur Wright College. The reason I decided to create a website for my project is because I wanted an outlet to be creative. While I want this website to be part of a portfolio, I still want it to feel like me and have that personality.

The idea was to create something that resembles pen, paper, sticky notes, and other stationary; sort of a scholarly, notebook vibe. I am still in the process of learning, so I want this website to be a jumping off point, something that can continue to be fixed and tweaked. I want the website to grow with me and my career.

I hope that you enjoy exploring the other pages and seeing what I have to offer. I hope to update the website semi-regularly, especially the blog section as I will be graduating and navigating the work field and finding a job. Thank you for being here!
